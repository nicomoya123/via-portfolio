Feature No. 04 — B2B Content Marketing

Four essays from MarketAcross.

Building brand presence, voice, and authority in the B2B space.

By Avia · Tel Aviv · 2025 – 2026

As the first content and social hire at MarketAcross, my job was to build the brand’s voice: define the tone, create a consistent presence, and develop a content strategy that made the company visible and recognizable. From thought leadership to evergreen content, every piece was designed to establish MarketAcross as a credible, authoritative name in the blockchain ecosystem.

From ghostwriting for executives to coordinating with staff across topics, managing new platforms, and getting the whole team aligned on content, this role pushed me to learn fast and deliver consistently. Blockchain and Web3 were entirely new territories, but the approach — narrative, curiosity, a high standard for quality — was the same one I bring to everything.

PR drives visibility, but content builds long-term trust and relevance. The strongest Web3 brands combine both into a consistent narrative system — not one-off moments.
